About Me

While teaching, I had the opportunity to put my life-long interest in programming to use by developing games and activities for use in my classroom. This opportunity led to starting my own freelance software business. Over the years, I have worked on projects with hundreds of clients from around the world using a variety of languages, frameworks and libraries. Now I want to share my excitement and love for programming with YOU!

Why learn to program?

As I'm sure you know, tech fields are some of the fastest and highest-paying professions you can pursue. This alone is a GREAT reason to consider becoming a software developer.

Learning to program also gives you a tool that can be applied to your own endeavors. Some professions that can be enhanced by knowing basic programming skills are researcher, mathmatician, engineer and entrepreneur.

My Teaching Philosophy

At the core of my philosophy is the idea that learning to learn is the key to creating a habit of continious improvement.

Whether you want to be a software developer, data engineer or some other tech-based professional, being able to understand and learn new technology is imperative. When you study with me, you will be learning the technical knowledge necessary to take a project from idea to fully functioning website. You will also learn how to approach learning new languages, frameworks and libraries.
